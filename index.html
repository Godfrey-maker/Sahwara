The Crisis

Geography Should Not Dictate Survival

In KwaZulu-Natal, 68% of roads are unpaved, potholed, and flood-prone. The physical backbone of rural healthcare is crumbling with devastating consequences.

72+ Hours

Routine delays for essential medicine restocking due to broken infrastructure and seasonal flooding

55%

Of livestock face vaccine and antibiotic shortages because veterinary posts are disconnected from supply depots

Blind

Disease surveillance is ineffective outbreaks are detected weeks too late, allowing zoonotic diseases to spread unchecked

How It Works

From Request to Delivery in Minutes

A seamless six-step process designed for rural healthcare workers with minimal technical literacy requirements.

01

Request

Clinic or veterinary post submits request via SMS, app, or dedicated hotline

02

Dispatch

AI platform optimizes route, consolidates multi-sector payloads, assigns nearest drone

03

Flight

Medical-grade UAV bypasses terrain and weather barriers autonomously

04

Delivery

Precision landing at designated zone with real-time tracking throughout
